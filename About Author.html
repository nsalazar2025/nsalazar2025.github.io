MEET THE AUTHOR

Nyah Kirsten B. Salazar, 14, a Philippine Science Highschool student during day, an anime-&-manga-centered magazine author at night. The author started watching anime during the start of quarantine and since then, she became an avid fan. She became intrigued by the unique plots and art styles of each anime she watched and each manga she read. Her top favorite genres are action, fantasy, romance, or a mix of all. Some of her favorite anime series include Jujutsu Kaisen, Tokyo Revengers, My Hero Academia, The Promised Neverland, Attack on Titan, Dr. Stone, Assassination Classroom, HunterxHunter and the list goes on!! As for anime movies, the famous movie Your Name is one of her favorites, including Hello World and Spirited Away! She also likes to read manga like Blue Lock, Gokurakugai Sanbandori no Ken, Haikyuu, Demon Slayer, and more! (both the latter having anime adaptions) The previously mentioned titles might already be familiar to most of you, as most have pretty big fanbases, which is why the author would like to recommend her other favorites that she thinks deserves more recognition!!
